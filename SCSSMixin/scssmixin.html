RWD Calc SCSS Mixin

Edited and enhanced by @scottkellum, original by @StuRobson. A SCSS mixin for my little RWD calculator.

Usage

Here is the mixin with all its variables, they are all optional and the default context is 960:

@include rwdcalc($target, $context, $margin, $padding, $border);

If you want to use box-sizing: border box just include this mixin at the top of your stack.

@include border-box;

The mixin only writes the code it needs, If you wish to pass variable padding, margins, or borders simply pass those lists through:

@include rwdcalc(230, 960, $padding: 10 20 10 5);

If you want pixels or a different kind of unit, label it. Only unitless values will be converted to match context.

// 20px padding remains a px value while 10 and 5 are converted to percent.
@include rwdcalc(230, 960, $padding: 10 20px 10 5);
